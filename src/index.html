Organizations are under a constant barrage of cyber threats, from ransomware to zero-day attacks and more. How does a next-gen, forward-thinking organization evolve to deal with these ever-changing cybersecurity threats?


Join us at our upcoming Learn and Lunch event to find out about the best new and effective strategies, preventative measures, and more. Lunch is included plus youll have the chance to chat about these challenges with experts and peers.

Join your local Sophos LA team for lunch and a discussion about driving threat prevention to unmatched levels. The artificial intelligence built into our Intercept X solution is a deep learning neural network, an advanced form of machine learning, that detects both known and unknown malware without relying on signatures.


This session will be a technical event with a deep dive into how we're keeping you protected against the latest threats including a live demonstration of our newest Deep Learning technology. We'll also show you how this technology integrates with the Sophos XG Firewall to provide an unmatched level of Synchronized Security for your organization.
